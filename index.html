Simple homemade ice cream recipie

recipie source

Ingredients:
1 gallon ziploc bag
1 quart ziploc bag
1 cup milk
1 tb sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/3 cup salt
ice

Preparation:
Pour milk, sugar and vanilla into the smaller, quart sized bag.
Fill the larger, gallon sized bag 3/4 full of ice, then add the salt to the ice.
Place smaller bag inside larger bag and close the seal tightly.
Shake bag for at least 5 minutes.
Eat!
